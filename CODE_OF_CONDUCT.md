# Code of Conduct

OmniTAK-iOS adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Summary

- Be welcoming, respectful, and patient
- Disagree on technical merits, not on people
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Assume good faith; ask questions before assigning malice

## Reporting

Conduct concerns: **j@engindearing.soy**

Reports are kept confidential. Project maintainers may issue warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal depending on severity.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — issue tracker, pull requests, discussions, chat, conferences — and to anyone representing the project in public.
